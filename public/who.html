Who am I?

I'm Linus Willner, but you can just call me LW if you meet me. On the internet, I commonly go under the moniker LWTech or LWTechGaming (My old username).

I'm 18 years old, currently on my final year in upper secondary school. As a person I'm an avid tech nerd that enjoys programming in his free time. In the past I've written and contributed to quite a few chatbots for the chat program Discord. Currently I'm mainly developing backend JavaScript applications and desktop programs in my leisure time.

When it comes to programming, I mostly just write code. However, I'm proficient in Finnish, Swedish and English, meaning I am also a proficient writer. As such, I often write documentation for the projects I maintain or contribute to. Some of the larger projects that I have done such things for include TheSharks, for which I have written lots of documentation and license texts, and translation projects, such as Discord's community-driven localization project.

I live in Loviisa, a small seaside town in Southern Finland. Within the next few years I'm expecting to move out in order to start studying in Helsinki and become an ICT engineer by degree, software developer by profession. I do all my coding work in a small home office, or optionally in the field as programming work doesn't always wait for one, even in the holiday season. I also strive to balance school and my hobby, both of which often take a lot of time out of my day.
