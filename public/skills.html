My skills

Skills by category

Writing

My native language is officially Swedish, but my proficiency in Finnish is native and my English isn't far from that. Writing longer texts isn't a problem for me. As such I find writing documentation and translation easy. My technical proficiency also allows me to write comprehensive technical texts.

I have provided my skills in writing and translation to multiple projects. Two of them are WildBeast and Bezerk. The former is a Discord chat bot, while the latter is a websocket manager.

Programming

I am proficient with JavaScript and Node.js. With Node.js I have created CLI programs, desktop applications and REST APIs. I also have experience in working with the Electron desktop application framework. I intend to expand my language knowledge in the near future to Java and Python.

Outside of programming, I'm familiar with several "meta-coding" practices. This includes Continuous Integration, version/source control (Git), semantic versioning, keeping a roadmap and managing a project.

General technical aptitude

I'm on my most proficient with Windows as a desktop environment, and I use it as my day-to-day OS. I can however understand UNIX operating systems (Specifically CLI), Linux and Mac in order of knowledge.

I have experience of system administration with remote Linux server environments. The operating systems I've worked with include Ubuntu (14.04 and 16.04) and Debian (Jessie, Wheezy). I'm comfortable with SSH environments and file transfer apps such as FileZilla.

Audio and video production

I am a somewhat proficient audiovisual content producer, I used to do YouTube as a hobby before programming. I have hobbyist experience with planning, scripting, recording and editing audiovisual content.

My program-specific knowledge includes mostly Adobe programs for this purpose, namely Premiere Pro, Audition, After Effects and Photoshop in order of knowledge.
